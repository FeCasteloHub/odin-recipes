Lasagna

Index Page

Description

Lasagna is a beloved Italian dish made from wide, flat pasta sheets layered with rich meat sauce—commonly a slow-cooked Bolognese—creamy béchamel sauce, and generous amounts of cheese, typically mozzarella and Parmesan. The layers are stacked and baked until hot and bubbly, with a golden crust on top. It’s a hearty and comforting meal, often enjoyed at family gatherings and special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles (or fresh lasagna sheets)
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups béchamel sauce (butter, flour, and milk)
  • Olive Oil
